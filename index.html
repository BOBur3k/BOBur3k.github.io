Data Science

My Data Science journey at The Knowledge House hones my skills in Python, data analysis, machine learning, SQL, and data visualization.

Empowering Students

My journey began at BMCC, where I served as a Math Tutor and Program Coordinator. In this role, I lit the academic spark within students, igniting their passion for learning.

At Guttman Community College, I continued my mission as an Academic Advisor, achieving a remarkable 90% passing rate for students under my guidance. I provided unwavering support and mentorship, ensuring their academic success.

My dedication to education also led me to City Tech, where I extended my academic guidance to over 50 students, helping them navigate the complexities of higher education.
